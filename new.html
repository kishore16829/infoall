About the Actor

The lead actor of GBU is Ajith Kumar, one of the most influential figures in Tamil cinema.

Remuneration: ₹100 Crore

Ajith Kumar

Ajith Kumar Subramaniam (born 1 May 1971) is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He has appeared in over 63 films and has received numerous awards, including Filmfare Awards South and Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.

Beyond cinema, Ajith is widely respected for his discipline, humility, and passion for motorsports.

Racing Career

Ajith Kumar is also a professional racing driver. He has competed in national and international FIA-recognized championships.

Formula BMW Asia – 2003

Ajith participated in the inaugural Formula BMW Asia Championship and completed the season with a 12th-place overall finish.

FIA 24H Series – 2025

After a 15-year break, Ajith returned to racing with his own team, Ajith Kumar Racing, securing third place and earning the Spirit of the Race recognition.

Film Career Overview

Ajith Kumar began his acting career in the early 1990s and gradually established himself as one of the leading actors in Tamil cinema. His breakthrough performance came with the film Vaalee (1999), where his dual-role portrayal received critical acclaim.

Known for selecting strong and unconventional scripts, Ajith has consistently reinvented himself through a wide range of roles, including romantic leads, action heroes, and intense character-driven performances.

Acting Style and Screen Presence

Ajith Kumar is widely admired for his restrained and realistic acting style. Unlike many commercial actors, he relies more on expressions and dialogue delivery rather than exaggerated gestures.

His screen presence, calm demeanor, and intense gaze have earned him a loyal fan base across generations. He is particularly known for his stylish performances in action and thriller genres.

Awards and Achievements

Over the course of his career, Ajith Kumar has received numerous awards recognizing his contribution to Tamil cinema. These include multiple Filmfare Awards South, Vijay Awards, and Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.

Apart from awards, he has also been featured multiple times in the Forbes India Celebrity 100 list, reflecting his popularity and commercial success.

Fan Following and Public Image

Ajith Kumar enjoys an immense fan following, particularly in Tamil Nadu and among Tamil-speaking audiences worldwide. His fans often refer to him as "Thala", a title that signifies respect and leadership.

Despite his stardom, Ajith maintains a low public profile and avoids unnecessary media exposure, which has further strengthened his image as a disciplined and humble personality.

Personal Life and Values

Ajith Kumar is known for leading a private and balanced personal life. He values discipline, family, and personal growth, and often emphasizes the importance of humility and hard work.

Beyond cinema and racing, he has a keen interest in photography and technology. His approach to life and career has inspired many young fans and aspiring artists.

Legacy and Influence

Ajith Kumar’s contribution to Tamil cinema extends beyond box-office success. He has influenced a generation of actors through his professionalism, discipline, and respect for the craft of filmmaking.

His journey as both an actor and a racing driver makes him a unique figure in Indian cinema, symbolizing passion, perseverance, and versatility.
